cross platform story telling: Meeting Point by Louis Macneice

Platform: Club Penguin

The poem recited at a Club Penguin coffee shop. I️ chose this platform because the poem takes place at a coffee shop and I️ thought it would be interesting to be in a digital version of that environment and be communicating with other penguins around me.

The poem read by the text-description feature on Tik Tok. The poem’s repetition of verses in combination with the theme of time portrays time as a looping measure. The nature of Tik Tok is similar, as videos on the app loop and continue to play until you exit. Both the poem and Tok Tok characterize time similarly.
